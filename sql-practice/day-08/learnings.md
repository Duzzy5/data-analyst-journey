# Day 08 Learnings

## What I Learned

* Aggregate functions summarize multiple rows into a single result.
* `COUNT()` returns the total number of rows.
* `SUM()` calculates the total of a numeric column.
* `AVG()` calculates the average value.
* `MAX()` returns the highest value.
* `MIN()` returns the lowest value.

## Confidence Check

✅ I can identify when to use aggregate functions.

✅ I understand the difference between detail queries and summary queries.

✅ I can answer business questions like:

* How many employees are there?
* What is the average salary?
* What is the highest salary?
* What is the total salary paid?

## Interview Takeaway

Aggregate functions are used to generate business insights by summarizing data instead of displaying every individual record.
